⛓Leash

Put your AI coding agent on a leash.

Approve commands, answer questions, and steer Claude Code from your iPhone, Apple Watch, and Mac — from the next room or the next city.

⬇ Download for Mac Get the iPhone app

Free Mac companion · macOS 14+ · notarized. The iPhone app pairs with it. You need both: the Mac app runs alongside Claude Code; the phone is your remote.

1

Install the Mac app

Open the DMG, drag Leash to Applications, launch it, and install the hook from the app.

2

Pair your iPhone

On the same Wi-Fi it connects automatically; off Wi-Fi, scan the QR or paste the pairing link.

3

Run Claude Code as usual

When it needs you, your phone buzzes. Approve, deny, or reply — from anywhere.
